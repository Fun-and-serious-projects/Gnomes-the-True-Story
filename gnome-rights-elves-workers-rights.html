The Great Toy-Making Segregation

For centuries, elves monopolized the toy-making industry, systematically excluding gnomes from meaningful workshop roles. Our people were relegated to menial tasks like "snow sweeping" and "beard trimming".

Our Groundbreaking Lawsuit: Gnomes v. North Pole Enterprises

In 2017, we filed a landmark case challenging the discriminatory hiring practices of the toy industry. Our key arguments:

  • Gnomes possess superior craftsmanship skills
  • Our smaller hands are PERFECT for intricate toy mechanisms
  • We demand equal representation in toy production

Victory: Integrated Toy Workshops

In 2019, we achieved a historic breakthrough. Now, gnomes work alongside elves in ALL toy-making departments:

  • Mechanical Engineering Division
  • Quality Control
  • Design and Prototyping
  • Executive Management

Note to elves: Yes, we're better at your job. No, we're not sorry.
