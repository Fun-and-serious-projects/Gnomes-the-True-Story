Mandatory Gnome Likeness Licensing (2020)

Manufacturers must now obtain official approval before producing gnome figurines, ensuring respectful representation.

Garden Placement Rights Act (2015)

Gnomes can no longer be positioned without their consent. Each gnome now has the right to choose their garden location.

Annual Gnome Day Proclamation (1995)

Successfully lobbied for an official day of recognition, celebrated worldwide on the summer solstice.

Anti-Stereotyping Campaign (Ongoing)

Combating harmful myths like "gnomes are lazy" and "gnomes only exist to decorate gardens".
